CONTROLS

IN MENU:
Navigate through menu using ARROW KEYS.
Press ENTER to select.

IN GAME:
Use ARROW KEYS to move the character.
Press SPACE to plant the bomb.
Press M to mute/unmute music.
Press N to mute/unmute sound effects.
Press X to toggle cheatmode.
Press P to pause.

GAME DESCRIPTION

Welcome to our bomberman game, made by Thomas and Gerol! Game objective is simple: defeat the bots and find escape portal in under 3 minutes and you win! But wait, theres a secret bonus objective: find the apple hidden in one of the brickwalls for extra points!

Scoring: Defeating each bot awards 100 points and finding the missing apple gives 200 points.

Good luck and have fun!
